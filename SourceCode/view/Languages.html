Overview

The languages course explores the relation of language to thought and imagination, both through the study of the essential elements of language and through the translation and analysis of literary and philosophical texts. The examination of word classes and grammatical features raises questions about the ways language articulates meaning, while the consideration of logical, rhetorical, and poetic structures encourages reflection on the ways it can convince, persuade, and affect us.
